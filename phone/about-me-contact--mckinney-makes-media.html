"New Thought is Born of the Curious"

   I am Luke McKinney, a Kansas City, MO based multimedia artist.  Constantly saying "Yes" to new opportunities defined a path that has taught me a unique skill set.  Those skills allow me to work effectively in a variety of areas implementing creative solutions and content specific approaches.

 

"You Must Know What's Behind You to Know What's Ahead"

    I grew up one hundred miles North, from where I currently live, on a small Iowa mink and hog farm, until the fur market collapsed.  We then moved to town, and my father started a small carpentry business.  Watching my father during that transition showed me a way of life that provided the freedom to turn passion and skill into a sustainable way of life, despite external factors out of our control.

 

"You're Only Truly Free to Choose Your Attitude and Your Ethic"

   Growing up in a rural Iowan community instilled me with a very traditional ethic; "Treat others as you'd want to be treated"; "You're only as good as your word"; "Hard work often pays off".  I love being a freelance artist because it gives me the ability to conduct business with an ethic I can stand behind. Conducting business this way makes it easy to stay in a great mood and make every project a positive and successful experience.  I'd rather have fun and get paid to play, rather than work.  Wouldn't you?

 

"Art Can Be In Any Form as Long as It Has Head, Heart, and Hands"

   I believe artistry comes through intention and execution. That being said, I know my clients put a lot of thought, a lot of themselves, into their projects.  It is my job to execute, with great craft and care, to elevate their projects to something more than even they maybe saw possible.  When you put that much of yourself into something, why not push it to something that deserves to be noticed?

 

"If Everybody Eats, Everybody's Happy"

  I know that I can't handle every project and every deadline on my own.  I love that because I love collaborating.  Kansas City is such a great creative base because of the local community.  The population of this community can easily support a project of any scale while utilizing individuals with specific styles to match your concept.  With so many freelancers, I can build a team based on what best suits your needs.

 

"It is Better to Fail, Than Not to Try"

   I believe there is a quality solution for just about every budget and deadline.  I think it is both of those factors that can help hone in an idea and keep it from getting too over-worked, over-thought, and over-complicated.  Please feel free to reach out to me so we can try to find a solution to meet your needs.
